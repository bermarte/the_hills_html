Taiwan Indonesia Vietnam Saudi-Arabia
Generous like its 23 million people, Taiwan offers wondrous vistas, lively traditions and a culture as luxuriant as Jade Mountain on a sunny day. It's easy to wander around all day and discover something new on the same path the very next day.
Made up of thousands of volcanic islands, Indonesia has many marvels hidden among them. From the surfer's paradise of Ubud with it's vibrant nightlife to the monkey Island of Ubu, you can be certain that one of these islands is a perfect fit for you.
Vietnam has touristic hot spots for various differing interests. In the capital there is a War Remnants Museum that shows how war has shaped modern Vietnam. The Ha Long Bay is the destination to go if you prefer your vacations in a more natural fashion and in Hôi An you can find historical towns.
Saudi Arabia is home to the holy city of Mecca and the Kaaba, the most important city and location for Islam. You can also find the prophet's burial place in Medina. In Jeddah there is also the splendid Fountain of King Fadh and the old city centre.
