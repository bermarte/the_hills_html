1. Summary

Alcatraz has been a metal festival for the young since 2009. It has relaxed metal atmosphere and attracts fans of all ages. Thanks to the good reputation it has earned through its versatile line-up and excellent location, a new stage was added in 2010. They also make a point of including performances by Belgian bands.
